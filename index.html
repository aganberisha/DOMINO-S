About Us

The first German Domino's store opened in Berlin in 2011. Following the acquisition of Joey's Pizza in 2016 and Hallo Pizza in 2018, Domino's Pizza is now the number 1 pizza home delivery in Germany with over 350 stores and is one of the ten largest gastronomic franchisors.
